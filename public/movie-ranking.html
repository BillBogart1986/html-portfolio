The Best Movies According to Bill B

My top 3 movies of all-time

Close Encounters Of The Third Kind

I love the science fiction aspect of it. I thought Steven Spealberg did an awesome job writing, directing, and making this movie. It even had a Cameo from J Allen Hynik.

Jaws

This movie is the perfect thriller. It has suspense and anticipation and doesn't have too much gore.

Independence Day

Another science fiction that I love. It has an aspect of software development which is in my wheel house.
